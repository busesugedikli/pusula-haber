Innovation and Applied Learning in Education
The university not only provides theoretical knowledge to its students, but also offers the opportunity to reinforce this knowledge with applied projects. At Pusula Academy, students work on real-world projects to create their personal portfolios and better understand the dynamics of the media industry.

Pusula Academy: The Door to the Media World
Pusula Academy helps students prepare for the situations they will encounter in the real world by combining theory and practice. In this way, graduates are equipped not only with academic knowledge but also with practical experience to compete in the business world.

Pusula News Site: News from Students
Pusula News Site, as an extension of Pusula Academy, offers students applied journalism experience. This site is a dynamic platform where students publish news, videos and interviews. The news site allows students to present their content to a real audience and receive feedback. This helps students improve by seeing their own mistakes and produce better content.
