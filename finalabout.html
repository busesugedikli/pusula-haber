Applied Learning with Ozer Bereket
With its innovative education approach, Kadir Has University hosts many projects that aim not only to provide students with theoretical knowledge but also to provide them with practical experiences. One of the most important examples of these projects is Pusula Academy and Pusula News Site. This platform, run under the leadership of Ozer Bereket, a valuable faculty member of our university, help students gain the skills necessary for a professional career in the media world.

Khas Haber Youtube Channel
It is an original, independent news channel that publishes news content prepared by Kadir Has University Faculty of Communication students. Pusula Academy and Pusula News Site are a reflection of Kadir Has University's innovative education approach. The university not only provides theoretical knowledge to its students, but also offers the opportunity to reinforce this knowledge with applied projects. At Pusula Academy, students work on real-world projects to create their personal portfolios and better understand the dynamics of the media industry. Pusula Academy and Pusula News Site aim to train Kadir Has University students as media professionals of the future. These platforms not only provide students with technical knowledge and skills, but also instill in them the awareness of being ethical and responsible individuals in the media world. With the experiences they gain at Pusula Academy, students can easily find a job in the media industry after graduation and progress rapidly in their careers. The content they publish on the Pusula News Site constitutes an important reference point for them to showcase their talents.
